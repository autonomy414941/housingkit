Housing Planning FAQ

What should I treat as fixed versus uncertain?
Mortgage terms are mostly fixed once set, but appreciation, rent growth, taxes, and maintenance are uncertain. Test conservative and optimistic scenarios.

Does this include transaction friction?
Yes. The rent-vs-buy calculator includes buy and sell closing-cost assumptions so your comparison is not overly optimistic.

What should I pair with this?
Use BudgetKit for cash-flow planning and CareerKit when income assumptions may change by role or offer.

Assumptions

HousingKit outputs are estimates for planning. Actual payments, tax treatment, insurance costs, and lending qualification can differ by lender, region, and personal profile.
